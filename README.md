### 2. Know the Formulas

**Load Calculations:**
- General lighting: Sq ft × 3 VA (dwelling)
- Continuous load: 125% of load
- Optional calculation: 10k@100% + remainder@40%

**Grounding:**
- GEC size: Based on largest service conductor (250.66)
- EGC size: Based on OCPD rating (250.122)

### 3. Common Exam Topics

## Quick Reference Values

### Working Space (110.26)

| Voltage | Depth |
|---------|-------|
| 0-150V | 3 ft |
| 151-600V | 3 ft |
| 601V+ | 4-5 ft |

**Width:** 30 in minimum or equipment width  
**Height:** 6.5 ft minimum

### Receptacle Spacing (210.52)

- **General:** No point more than 6 ft from receptacle
- **Kitchen counters:** No point more than 24 in from receptacle
- **Bathrooms:** Within 3 ft of basin

### Service Ratings (230.79)

| Occupancy | Minimum Rating |
|-----------|----------------|
| One-family dwelling | 100A |
| All others | 60A |

### Grounding Electrodes (250.52)

| Type | Minimum Size/Length |
|------|---------------------|
| Rod | 8 ft length, 5/8 in diameter |
| Pipe | 8 ft length, ¾ in diameter |
| Plate | 2 ft² surface area |
| Ground ring | 20 ft of 2 AWG bare copper |
| Concrete-encased | 20 ft of ½ in rebar or 4 AWG copper |
